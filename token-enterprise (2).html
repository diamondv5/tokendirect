The Problem · 行业现状

中转站的隐患，
远比你想象的严重。 The risks of proxy relay services are far greater than you think.

对于个人用户，中转站或许无伤大雅。但对于大型企业、上市公司而言，数据安全是一条不可逾越的红线。然而，许多企业对 AI Token 产品的认知仍停留在初级阶段，尚未意识到供给链路中潜藏的巨大风险。 For individual users, proxy relay services may be harmless. But for large enterprises and publicly listed companies, data security is a non-negotiable line. Yet many enterprises still have only a surface-level understanding of AI token products, unaware of the significant risks hidden in the supply chain.

⛓️‍💥

上游不透明 Opaque Upstream Supply

中转站虽对外宣称不存储数据，但其真正的上游供给方是谁，往往不得而知。企业数据可能在未知的第三方之间流转。Relay providers claim they don't store data, but the true upstream supplier is often unknown. Enterprise data may flow through unverified third parties.

💳

资源来源存疑 Questionable Token Sources

价格异常低廉的供应商，其 Token 资源池可能来自信用卡盗刷、平台漏洞薅羊毛等灰色渠道，产品线生命周期极短。Unusually cheap providers may source tokens from stolen credit cards, platform exploits, or other gray-market channels — with extremely short product lifecycles.

🚪

供应商跑路风险 Vendor Flight Risk

非官方供给链路中，客户随时面临供应商突然消失、服务中断、数据无法追溯的风险，直接威胁业务连续性。In unofficial supply chains, customers face the constant risk of vendors disappearing overnight — leading to service outages and untraceable data, directly threatening business continuity.
